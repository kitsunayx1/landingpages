How It Works
Simple when it matters most
No manual data entry. No guessing. Afterlight finds what you have and tells your family exactly what to do.
1

Scan your devices

Our desktop scanner reads your saved passwords, email receipts, and browser history to build a complete inventory of every account you have. Everything stays local on your machine.

2

Add your wishes

Review what we found, assign beneficiaries, and leave instructions. Tell your family who to contact, what to claim, and what to cancel. All encrypted, only accessible by who you choose.

3

Your family is guided

When the time comes, your designated person receives a prioritized checklist with vendor-specific closure instructions, contact numbers, and required documents for every account.
