Intelligence professionals and analysts needed a text processing tool that respects operational constraints — not one that assumes cloud connectivity is acceptable.

UMBRA integrates into macOS Services, available in any app's right-click menu when text is selected. No copy-paste workflow. No switching windows. Select, right-click, done.
