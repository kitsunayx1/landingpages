Why We Built This

The whole publishing stack. One app. One purchase.

Every creator and marketer ends up paying five different SaaS tools to do one job. PULSE puts it all under one roof — and keeps it on your machine.

01

No SaaS subscription for your own content

Hootsuite, Buffer, and Semrush charge per seat per month. PULSE is a local app. One purchase. Your data lives in ~/Library, not their server.

02

Both broadcast AND marketing in one window

Stop switching between your scheduling tool, your SEO tool, and your analytics dashboard. PULSE is the whole stack, unified in a single SwiftUI window.

03

Your Google credentials, your data

GSC and GA4 connections use your own Google OAuth tokens. PULSE reads your data; it never touches GIL servers. You own the connection.

04

AI recommendations without AI billing

ENVOY-style recommendations run locally. No monthly AI subscription. Bring your own key if you want cloud model quality — but the default works offline.

Marketing Workspace

GSC + GA4 Views

Expose a local API endpoint for automation, scripting, and integration with your existing tools. No cloud relay. All traffic stays on localhost.
