Why we built this

Government paperwork deserves better tools.

01
Government documents deserve a dedicated home

FOIA requests, benefit applications, meeting minutes, public comments — these don't fit cleanly into a notes app or a file folder. CiviBridge gives them a structured home with the metadata they need: status, agency, deadlines, and response tracking.

02
Track your requests — every one of them

Know exactly where every records request stands, what's outstanding, and when to follow up. CiviBridge surfaces overdue requests, tracks acknowledgment letters, and keeps a full timeline for every open matter.

03
Your data is yours

CiviBridge stores everything locally. No government data goes through a GIL server. Your FOIA requests, benefit documents, and civic correspondence never touch the cloud unless you choose to back them up yourself.

04
Built for engaged citizens

Whether you're tracking your city council's voting record, navigating federal benefits, or filing your first public records request, CiviBridge makes the process manageable. No prior experience required.

Features

Every tool the informed citizen needs.

📑
Document Manager

A structured library for all your civic documents — FOIA responses, benefit letters, meeting agendas, and public comments. Tagged, searchable, and organized by agency and date.

📬
FOIA Request Tracker

Log requests to any federal, state, or local agency. Track submission dates, acknowledgment deadlines, response status, and appeal windows. Never miss a follow-up date again.

🏛
Meeting Calendar

Track city council, school board, planning commission, and other local government meetings. Add agendas, minutes, and your own notes. Follow items across meetings over time.

📋
Benefit Navigator

Guided workflows for common federal and state benefit applications. Know what documents you need, where to submit, and how to track your application status — all in plain language.

🔍
Document Search

Instant full-text search across all your civic documents. Find any letter, response, or agenda item in milliseconds — across agencies, dates, and document types.

💾
Local-Only Storage

All documents and request data are stored in your local file system. Back up with Time Machine, organize with Finder. CiviBridge never transmits your civic data anywhere.
