Features
Everything your claim needs, organized
Stop digging through folders and filing cabinets. Veritas brings it all together and shows you what's missing.
◈

Record Vault

Upload and organize all your medical records, service treatment records, DD-214, buddy statements, and nexus letters in one encrypted vault.

◇

Health Log

Daily symptom tracking with date-stamped entries. Document flare-ups, pain levels, and how conditions affect your daily life. Creates a continuous evidence trail.

⬡

Condition Tracker

Track each claimed condition with linked evidence, service connection documentation, and claim strength assessment. See exactly what you have and what you still need.

□

Evidence Checklist

Per-condition checklists showing required documents — diagnosis, nexus letter, buddy statements, service records. Know what's missing before you file.

Appointment Prep

Before your C&P exam, Veritas surfaces your relevant records, symptom history, and talking points so you walk in prepared, not scrambling.

○

Export Package

Generate an organized evidence package with table of contents, condition summaries, and linked documents. Hand it to your VSO or submit it yourself.
