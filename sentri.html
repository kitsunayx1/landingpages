Why We Built This

Your audit logs already
know. SENTRI listens.

Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace log everything — but most organizations never look at those logs until something goes wrong. SENTRI makes the patterns visible in real time, without requiring a SIEM deployment or a dedicated security team.

01 /
No data leaves your machine

SENTRI fetches audit logs from Microsoft or Google and stores them locally. Nothing goes to GIL servers. No SIEM subscription required. Your organization's data stays inside your organization.

02 /
Catches real insider risk patterns

Bulk downloads, external email forwarding, impossible travel, admin role changes, abnormal sharing activity — the patterns that matter to HR, legal, and IT, not raw event counts.

03 /
Built for teams without a security team

Designed for IT managers, HR, and legal stakeholders. Clear risk scores and plain-English findings — not raw log data. You shouldn't need a security analyst to read a report.

04 /
Free forever, open source

The full platform is open source and will stay free. No seat licenses. No enterprise contracts. Inspect the code, fork it, self-host it. We believe this capability should be accessible to every organization.

Features

From log ingestion
to actionable intelligence.

Critical — Bulk exfiltration detected
Elevated — Unusual forwarding rule
Nominal — Activity within baseline
🔍
Audit Log Ingestion

OAuth-authenticated pull from Microsoft 365 Unified Audit Log and Google Workspace Admin SDK. Logs stored locally in SQLite — no cloud relay.

⚠️
Anomaly Detection

Rules-based and statistical detection for bulk downloads, impossible travel, external forwarding, admin escalations, and abnormal access patterns.

👤
Per-User Risk Scoring

Composite risk score per user based on signal type, severity, and recency. Drill down from score to individual events in one click.

📊
Risk Dashboard

Organization-wide risk view with trend lines, top flagged users, signal distribution, and time-range filtering. Designed for daily review in under 5 minutes.

📋
Incident Reports

One-click incident reports ready for HR, legal, or management — plain English, timestamped, with full event chain. Export to PDF or markdown.

🔗
M365 + Google Workspace

Supports both Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace in the same instance. Switch tenants, compare activity, manage multiple orgs from one dashboard.
