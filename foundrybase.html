Why FoundryBase exists

Every SaaS tool for small
businesses wants your
data in their cloud.

QuickBooks, HoneyBook, Notion, Airtable — they're all useful products. They're all building profiles of your business. They're all subscription costs that compound. And none of them were built for a solo operator running an LLC that does government contracting, software development, and consulting simultaneously.

FoundryBase was built by a solo LLC operator who was frustrated with the choice between 12 different tools or one bloated platform that didn't fit the workflow. It runs locally, it works offline, it keeps your business records on your machine where they belong. The audit trail is yours. The data is yours. No vendor can lock you out.

Institutional memory

Every project, receipt, meeting, contract, and communication is logged with a full audit trail. Before/after payloads on every mutation. Your business history is searchable and exportable.

Government contracting built in

Solicitations, bids, awards, admin timelines, checklists, and packet exports. SDVOSB-specific fields. DUNS tracking. Built by a veteran-owned small business, for veteran-owned small businesses.

Accountant-ready exports

Generate tax-ready packets for your CPA. CSV and JSON exports across all modules. ZIP bundles with documents attached. SBIR funding report templates.

No internet required

Everything except optional Plaid banking sync runs 100% offline. Works on a plane. Works in a SCIF. Works when your internet is down on a deadline.
