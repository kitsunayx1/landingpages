Why We Built This

One AI voice isn't
a review. It's an opinion.

Serious documents deserve serious scrutiny. A security audit, a patent claim, a contract, a piece of code — these don't need a single AI response. They need a room full of experts who disagree with each other. Council builds that room.

01 /
Not a chatbot

Traditional AI chat gives you one perspective. Council runs a full debate across multiple specialized minds before surfacing findings. The verdict emerges from disagreement, not a single answer.

02 /
Built for serious review

Security audits, code reviews, patent claims, legal risk analysis, manuscript critiques. Council was built for documents that matter — not summarization, not Q&A.

03 /
You own the API keys

Each persona runs on a model you choose via OpenRouter. Council never bills you for model usage or stores your documents. Your API keys, your models, your data.

04 /
Free forever for core use

The free council gives you 7 personas on zero-cost models. No credit card, no trial period. Upgrade for specialized councils, unlimited sessions, and the full persona library.
