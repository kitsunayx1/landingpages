Why Galley exists

A missed allergy at sea
isn't a complaint.
It's an emergency.

Yacht chefs work in one of the most demanding food service environments in the world. New guests every charter, different allergies and restrictions every trip, remote ports with limited provisioning, no prep kitchen, no sous chef.

The tools most chefs use for this — Notes, spreadsheets, a notebook in the galley — aren't built for it. There's no existing app that combines guest allergy profiles, AI-powered menu generation, and inventory management in a single place designed for maritime conditions. Galley was built because that gap is a safety risk, not just an inconvenience.

Safety by design

Allergy severity is the primary organizing concept — anaphylactic risks surface first, always. The allergy checker is available from any screen, one tap. Every menu generation prompt includes the full guest restriction set. You can't accidentally forget a guest's allergy because the app won't let you.

Charter-as-context

Everything in Galley — guests, menus, inventory — is scoped to the active charter. Switching to the next charter gives you a clean slate. Guest history stays available but doesn't pollute the current trip's data.

Works when connectivity doesn't

Built offline-first for the reality of anchoring in remote bays with no signal. Read-only access works immediately. Full functionality restores when you're back in range.

AI that actually helps

Select a meal type, cuisine, and date. Galley sends all guest restrictions to Claude and returns a menu that respects every constraint. Not a generic recipe — a menu generated specifically for your current guest manifest.

Features

Everything in one galley.

👥
Guest profiles

Full allergy severity tracking (anaphylactic / intolerant / preference), diet type, dislikes, cuisine preferences. Real-time allergen summary for the active charter.

🤖
AI menu generation

Generate menus that automatically respect every guest's restrictions. Powered by Claude. Works with your actual guest manifest, not sample data.

⚡
Allergy checker

Enter any dish and its ingredients. Get instant SAFE / WARNING / CONFLICT status against all current guests. Floating — accessible from anywhere in the app.

📦
Inventory tracking

Log provisions by category: proteins, produce, dairy, dry goods, beverages, frozen. Adjust quantities on the fly. Know what you have before you provision.

⛵
Charter management

Create and switch between charters. Upcoming, active, completed. Each charter is a self-contained context — guests, menus, and inventory scoped to the trip.

📴
Offline support

Read-only access works without connectivity. Full functionality when online. Built for the reality of remote anchorages and limited mobile signal.
