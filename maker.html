Why MakerOS

Ravelry is a directory.
You need a studio.

Ravelry is brilliant for discovering patterns. But it was never designed to manage your actual craft life — your stash, your active projects, your row counts, your timer, your gift list.

Most crafters end up with Ravelry for patterns, a note app for projects, a spreadsheet for stash, and paper for row counts. MakerOS brings all of it under one roof, built specifically for how crafters actually work.

📦
Your stash, actually organized

Log yarn by weight, fiber, dye lot, and yardage. Scan barcodes to auto-fill brand and category. Know exactly what you have before you buy more — or before you start a project and run short.

🎯
Stash-to-pattern matching

Link pattern materials to your stash items. MakerOS tells you if you have enough, what you're short on, and generates a shopping list for the rest. No more guessing mid-project.

⏱
Time tracking that persists

Start a timer on any project. Switch apps. Put your phone down. The timer keeps running and the time accumulates correctly. See how many hours you've actually spent crafting this year.

🎁
Gift tracking built in

Tag projects with a recipient. Your dashboard shows active gifts at a glance. Never show up to a birthday having forgotten you were making something for someone.

Features

Built around
how you actually craft.

🧶 Project tracking

Status management, progress tracking, linked supplies, timer, gift recipient. Completion celebration with confetti and social sharing.

📱 Barcode scanner

Scan yarn labels to auto-fill name and brand. 17 major yarn brands recognized. Never type Malabrigo Rios again.

🔢 Row counter

Multiple named counters. Haptic feedback. Screen wake lock so it doesn't go dark mid-row. Exactly what you need from a counter.

📚 Pattern library

Save patterns with material requirements. Link materials to your stash. Stash readiness indicator before you cast on.

🌙 Dark mode

Warm dark theme using deep browns and cream — not cold grey. Crafting at night shouldn't hurt your eyes.

📴 Works offline

Everything stored on your device. No account needed to use the app. Your craft data is yours.
