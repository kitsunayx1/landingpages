Why CARBON Exists

Every TIP wants
your data in their cloud.

Recorded Future, Mandiant Advantage, ThreatConnect — they're powerful platforms built for enterprise teams with enterprise budgets and enterprise data agreements. If you're an independent analyst, a small SOC team, or a security researcher, you're paying for seats you don't need and sending your intelligence to a third-party server you can't audit.

CARBON was built because the analyst running a solo threat research operation deserves the same capability without the SaaS tax. Everything runs locally. Your IOC database, your actor profiles, your reports — SQLite on your disk. No telemetry. No account required for core functionality. Bring your own API keys for the enrichment services you already use.

01 /
No cloud lock-in

Your intelligence database is a SQLite file on your machine. Export it, back it up, move it — it's yours. No vendor can turn off your access to your own research.

02 /
Bring your own keys

AI analysis, feed enrichment, HIBP breach monitoring — all work with your own API keys. CARBON is the orchestration layer, not the billing layer.

03 /
Built for real workflows

MITRE ATT&CK auto-loads on first run. Five public feeds ingest immediately. Reports export to PDF, markdown, and plain text for email distribution.

04 /
Open source core

The platform that does IOC management, actor tracking, and breach monitoring is open source. Paid features add automation and intelligence on top — not gating the fundamentals.

Capabilities

Everything an analyst
actually needs.

⚡
IOC Management

Ingest from 5 public feeds or add manually. Confidence scoring, staleness decay, enrichment via AbuseIPDB, VirusTotal, Shodan.

🎯
Actor Tracking

MITRE ATT&CK groups auto-loaded. Track actors, build rich intelligence profiles, score relevance against your organization's profile.

📋
Intelligence Reports

Four report templates. Split editor with live markdown preview. TLP classification. Export to PDF, markdown, plain text for email.

🔍
Breach Monitoring

Monitor your domains against HaveIBeenPwned automatically every 6 hours. Severity scoring, data type classification, scan history.

🤖
AI Integration

Works with Claude, OpenAI, or a local LM Studio instance. Actor analysis, report generation, threat summaries — your key, your model.

🗺
ATT&CK Matrix

800+ threat groups, 600+ techniques. Visualize TTP coverage, map actors to tactics, identify detection gaps in your security program.
