Why We Built This

Because care is complicated — your tools shouldn't be.

Managing care for a parent, a child with complex needs, or a family member in recovery involves more coordination than any text thread or shared note can hold.

01

Care is a team sport

Managing care for a parent or a child with complex needs involves multiple family members, caregivers, and providers. FamilyCare keeps everyone on the same page — without the group text chaos.

02

Everything in one place

Medication schedules, care notes, upcoming appointments, shared documents, emergency contacts. No more searching through texts and emails when you need something fast.

03

Designed for non-technical users

Features

Everything a caregiving family needs, finally together.

From daily medication reminders to document storage for medical records — FamilyCare brings the full caregiving workflow into one accessible, shared space.

📅

Shared Calendar

A shared family calendar for appointments, care visits, medication pickups, and therapy sessions. Everyone sees the same schedule — no more double-bookings or missed visits.

💊

Medication Tracker

Track medications, dosages, schedules, and refill dates for every family member. Log when medications are given and get reminders before refills run out.

📝

Care Notes

A shared log for care observations, behavioral notes, and updates from providers. Every caregiver sees the same record — no information falls through the cracks.

📄

Document Storage

Store medical records, insurance cards, care plans, legal documents, and emergency contacts. Accessible to authorized family members from any device, any time.

👥

Multi-Caregiver Access

Invite family members, professional caregivers, and care coordinators with role-based access. Everyone sees what they need — and nothing they shouldn't.

🔔

Task Reminders

Assign care tasks with due dates and reminders. Know what's done, what's pending, and who's responsible — without another group text asking if anyone gave the medication.
