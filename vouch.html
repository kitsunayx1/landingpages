eBay, Facebook Marketplace, Depop — condition disputes cost sellers money and time. Vouch creates a neutral, independently verifiable record before any item ships.

02

Cryptographic seals, not screenshots

Vouch creates a tamper-evident hash at recording time. The record cannot be altered after the seal is made — screenshots can be faked, a sealed hash cannot.

Neither party controls the evidence. Vouch holds it as a neutral intermediary until the transaction resolves. No editing, no deleting, no he-said-she-said.

Platform Features

Every layer of proof.
Built in.

From recording to resolution — Vouch handles the full chain of custody for every transaction.

📹

Condition Recording

In-app guided recording flow for item condition at time of packaging. Timestamped, geotagged, and immediately sealed.

🔐

Cryptographic Sealing

SHA-256 hash generated at capture time. Stored in a write-once ledger. The seal proves when the recording was made and that it hasn't been altered.

🏦

Neutral Escrow

Vouch holds the sealed evidence as an independent third party. Neither buyer nor seller can modify or delete the record.

✅

Transaction Verification

Link a Vouch record to a specific transaction. Share a verification link with buyers or platforms during a dispute process.

⚖️

Dispute Support

When a dispute is filed, Vouch provides a formatted evidence package: timestamped video, hash certificate, and chain-of-custody log.

📱

iOS + Android

Native apps for both platforms. Record, seal, and manage transactions from your phone — right at the packing table.
