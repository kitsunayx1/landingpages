Features
Everything your LLC needs in one place
Stop duct-taping QuickBooks, spreadsheets, and sticky notes. FoundryBase is purpose-built for how you actually run a business.
◈

Client Management

Full client profiles with contact info, contract history, communication log, and lifetime revenue. Know every client at a glance.

◇

Project Tracking

Track every engagement from proposal to payment. Status lifecycle, milestones, deliverables, and linked time entries. Nothing falls through the cracks.

□

Time Logging

Log billable hours by client and project. Timer or manual entry. Auto-calculates totals for invoicing and tracks utilization rate.

△

Invoicing & Payments

Generate invoices from time logs and project milestones. Track payment status. Know who owes you and how overdue they are.

○

Expense Tracking

Log business expenses with categories, receipts, and tax deductibility flags. Import bank transactions or enter manually. Every dollar accounted for.

◆

Tax Prep Export

End of year, one click. Revenue by client, deductible expenses by category, quarterly estimates, 1099 data. Hand it to your accountant or self-file with confidence.

▷

Gov Contract Tracking

Track government contracts with SAM.gov data, contract numbers, period of performance, CLINs, and deliverable schedules. Built for the compliance you need.

☆

Document Vault

Store contracts, SOWs, NDAs, insurance certs, and licenses. Link documents to clients and projects. Everything findable in seconds.

⬡

Dashboard & Reports

Real-time P&L, revenue trends, client concentration, aging receivables, and utilization. Know the health of your business at all times.

Every expense categorized automatically

As you log expenses throughout the year, FoundryBase categorizes them by tax-relevant categories. No year-end scramble to sort receipts.

1099 data ready for every client

Revenue by client, by project, by month. Whether you need to issue 1099s or report income, the data is already organized.

Quarterly estimate tracking

FoundryBase calculates estimated quarterly tax payments based on your year-to-date revenue and expenses. Never miss a payment or overpay.

One-click accountant export

Generate a complete tax package — P&L, expense reports, client revenue summary, receipt archive. Your accountant will thank you.
