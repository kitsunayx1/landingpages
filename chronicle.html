Why we built this

Writing apps shouldn't require a subscription.

01
No subscription to write your own documents

Chronicle is a one-time purchase. No monthly fee, no recurring charge to access your own files. Your documents are yours indefinitely — whether or not you're online, whether or not the company exists in five years.

02
A template library that actually helps

Start from professionally designed templates — business letters, proposals, reports, agreements, invoices, and more. The onboarding wizard interviews you briefly and picks the right starting point for what you're writing.

03
DOCX export that works everywhere

Send documents anywhere. Your Chronicle files export to standard .docx format that opens correctly in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, and LibreOffice. No proprietary lock-in, no conversion surprises.

04
Privacy by default

No cloud sync, no account required, no analytics SDK phoning home. Documents live in your local file system. Nothing is ever sent to any server. Chronicle is software, not a service.

Features

Every tool the thoughtful writer needs.

✍
Document Editor

A clean, distraction-free writing environment with rich text formatting, heading styles, lists, tables, and inline images. Designed for focus, not menus.

📄
Template Library

Curated collection of professionally designed templates across business, legal, creative, and personal categories. Searchable, filterable, and ready to use.

🎓
Onboarding Wizard

Answer a few questions about what you're writing and Chronicle selects the best-fit template, pre-fills common fields, and gets you to a first draft faster.

📤
DOCX Export

One-click export to .docx format with accurate style preservation — headings, fonts, spacing, and tables render correctly in Word, Google Docs, and LibreOffice.

🔍
Full-Text Search

Instant full-text search across your entire document library. Find any word or phrase across all your Chronicle files in milliseconds, without indexing delays.

💾
Local Storage

All documents are saved as standard files in your chosen folder. Organize with Finder, back up with Time Machine, sync with your own iCloud — Chronicle stays out of the way.
